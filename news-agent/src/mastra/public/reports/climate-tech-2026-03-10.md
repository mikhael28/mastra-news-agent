## Carbon Removal & Short‑Lived Pollutants  
*Suck it up and scrub it out: DAC breakthroughs, mineral tricks, and corporate crusades to evict CO2 and its nastier cousins.*

- **DAC Breakthrough Unlocks 3x Cheaper Carbon Removal Projects**  
  Sustaera reports a third‑generation, electrically powered DAC system that claims >90% energy efficiency using a nano‑structured sorbent with integrated resistive heating. If validated at scale, the approach could substantially lower DAC costs and expand viable removal project economics.  
  Source: portal.sina.com.hk — [Source](https://portal.sina.com.hk/finance/finance-globenewswire/globenewswire/2026/03/09/1593974/dac-breakthrough-unlocks-3x-cheaper-carbon-removal-projects/)

- **A more durable direct air capture approach: Electrified mineral‑based system resists oxygen and humidity**  
  Researchers unveiled an electrified, mineral‑based DAC technique designed to resist degradation from oxygen and humidity — two thorny problems for many sorbent systems. The electrochemical route promises improved durability and lower maintenance, important for long‑lived removal deployments.  
  Source: techxplore.com — [Source](https://techxplore.com/news/2026-02-durable-air-capture-approach-electrified.html)

- **Carbon Direct Releases Groundbreaking Paper Calling for Integration of Superpollutant Mitigation With Carbon Removal Strategy**  
  Carbon Direct’s new paper argues that pairing superpollutant mitigation (e.g., methane, black carbon) with long‑term carbon removal yields larger near‑term climate benefits than treating each separately. The report urges integrated program design and funding to maximize overall warming avoided.  
  Source: finance.yahoo.com — [Source](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/carbon-direct-releases-groundbreaking-paper-131000064.html)

- **NorthX Joins the Advance Carbon Removal Coalition as a Founding Member**  
  NorthX Climate Tech became a founding member of the Advance Carbon Removal Coalition, supporting Canada’s bid to grow a domestic carbon removal industry. The coalition (announced at Carbon Removal Day 2026) aims to mobilize financing and project activity to scale removals.  
  Source: northx.ca — [Source](https://northx.ca/announcements/northx-joins-the-advance-carbon-removal-coalition-as-a-founding-member)

- **Beyond Alliance: Leading Companies will direct $100m to Cut Superpollutants**  
  A coalition including Amazon, Google, JPMorgan Chase and others launched the Superpollutant Action Initiative, committing $100 million to curb methane, black carbon, and refrigerants. Targeted funding for short‑lived climate pollutants can deliver outsized near‑term temperature benefits alongside longer‑term decarbonization.  
  Source: www.prnewswire.com — [Source](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/beyond-alliance-leading-companies-will-direct-100m-to-cut-superpollutants-302700977.html)
